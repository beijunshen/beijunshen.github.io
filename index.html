Beijun Shen

Postdoctoral Researcher
Department of Mechanical Engineering
Columbia University

I am a postdoctoral researcher in mechanical engineering working on the mechanics and design of soft materials and architected material systems. My research combines experimental characterization, constitutive modeling, and computational implementation to understand and design materials with programmable large-deformation and rate-dependent behavior.

My current work spans architected soft materials, liquid crystal elastomers, multimaterial polymer systems, and mechanics-guided design of graded soft structures.

Research Interests

  • Architected soft materials
  • Liquid crystal elastomers
  • Nonlinear mechanics of soft materials
  • Viscoelasticity and viscoplasticity
  • Multimaterial polymer systems and graded soft materials
  • Constitutive modeling and computational mechanics

Research Vision

My long-term goal is to develop predictive mechanics frameworks for soft and multifunctional material systems by connecting material synthesis and manufacturing, experimental characterization, constitutive theory, and finite-element implementation. I am particularly interested in how microstructure, architecture, material composition, and gradients in properties can be used to achieve enhanced mechanical performance and new functionalities.
